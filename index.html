Baked With Passion Since Day One

Crumb & Co. started in a tiny home kitchen with one simple goal: bake the kind of cookie you'd drive across town for. Every recipe is tested, tweaked, and perfected by hand before it ever reaches your box.

We use only real butter, Belgian chocolate, and locally sourced ingredients — no shortcuts, no preservatives, just honest baking.
